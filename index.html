About Rozafa

Rozafa SeaFood is Tirana's most celebrated seafood destination, bringing the Adriatic's finest catch directly to your table. Our philosophy is simple: absolute freshness, culinary craftsmanship, and an atmosphere of understated luxury.

Every dish tells a story of the Mediterranean — from wild-caught lobster to the day's freshest fish. We let the quality of our ingredients speak for itself, which is why our menu focuses on flavor, not prices.

No fixed prices — only market-fresh excellence. Ask our maître d' for today's selection.
